eurostat package published in R Journal

A formal publication on the eurostat CRAN R package has now appeared on-line in R Journal.

Further details on installation and use are provided at the package homepage, tutorial and cheat sheet. Blog posts provide further and more advanced examples on the package use.

We are also collecting information about publications using the eurostat R package, suggestions are welcome.
